# Solutions

Brasidata delivers data governance solutions designed for the realities of construction projects: multiple stakeholders, changing schedules, mixed tooling, and high cost of rework.

## What we solve

### Siloed project information

- Align data across planning, site execution, procurement, finance, and operations
- Reduce manual reconciliation and inconsistent reporting

### Low data quality

- Establish rules, monitoring, and issue workflows
- Improve trust in KPIs used for cost, schedule, and productivity decisions

### No shared definitions

- Define a business glossary and canonical metrics
- Standardize domains (projects, activities, resources, contracts, suppliers, assets)

### AI without foundations

- Build governance, metadata, and lineage to support reliable AI
- Reduce model risk caused by inconsistent or undocumented data

## Typical outcomes

- One “single source of truth” view for executive reporting
- Faster reporting cycles with fewer disputes between areas
- Better readiness for analytics and AI across the portfolio
