# About

Brasidata is a specialized consultancy focused on data governance for the construction sector. We combine strategy, operations, and technical implementation to turn fragmented data into a trusted asset for decisions and AI.

## How we work

- Outcome-first: governance that improves decisions and execution, not paperwork
- Practical by design: standards and workflows that teams can actually operate
- Phased delivery: quick wins first, then scale with a roadmap

## What you can expect

- Clear ownership and accountability for key data domains
- Shared definitions for metrics and reports
- Better quality and traceability for analytics and AI
