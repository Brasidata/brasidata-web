## Frequently asked questions

<div class="bd-faq">

<details>
<summary>How can BIM implementation improve my project?</summary>
<p>BIM implementation provides specialized advisory and support to integrate BIM into projects and workflows. Our team ensures a successful transition, optimizing processes and improving efficiency for your project or development.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How does BIM planning and strategy work?</summary>
<p>We develop a tailored BIM strategy based on your company’s needs and goals. This includes defining standards, guidelines, and targets to ensure maximum efficiency and process standardization.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What is BIM modeling and how does it help?</summary>
<p>We create parametric 3D models representing all project elements, such as architecture, structure, and MEP systems. This provides a holistic, collaborative view, reducing errors and improving decision-making.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What are the benefits of BIM implementation?</summary>
<p>We coordinate BIM models across disciplines to prevent clashes and conflicts. This improves communication between teams and enables a more agile, integrated workflow, preventing issues before execution.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What types of analyses and simulations can be done with BIM?</summary>
<p>We use BIM models for several analyses, including clash detection, structural analysis, energy simulation, and people flow simulation.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What is BIM data management and why is it important?</summary>
<p>We implement BIM information management processes across the project lifecycle. This ensures disciplines share reliable data, improving decision-making and information traceability.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How does BIM improve planning and budgeting?</summary>
<p>We use BIM models to create more accurate, detailed budgets, enabling cost reduction, better planning, and higher predictability.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How does a BIM audit improve existing projects?</summary>
<p>A BIM audit reviews and analyzes BIM adoption in projects already in progress. We identify errors, improvement opportunities, and optimization points to align your project with best practices.</p>
</details>
