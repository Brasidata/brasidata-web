Frequently asked questions

How can BIM implementation improve my project?

BIM implementation provides specialized advisory and support to integrate BIM into projects and workflows. Our team ensures a successful transition, optimizing processes and improving efficiency for your project or development.

How does BIM planning and strategy work?

We develop a tailored BIM strategy based on your company’s needs and goals. This includes defining standards, guidelines, and targets to ensure maximum efficiency and process standardization.

What is BIM modeling and how does it help?

We create parametric 3D models representing all project elements, such as architecture, structure, and MEP systems. This provides a holistic, collaborative view, reducing errors and improving decision-making.

What are the benefits of BIM implementation?

We coordinate BIM models across disciplines to prevent clashes and conflicts. This improves communication between teams and enables a more agile, integrated workflow, preventing issues before execution.

What types of analyses and simulations can be done with BIM?

We use BIM models for several analyses, including clash detection, structural analysis, energy simulation, and people flow simulation.

What is BIM data management and why is it important?

We implement BIM information management processes across the project lifecycle. This ensures disciplines share reliable data, improving decision-making and information traceability.

How does BIM improve planning and budgeting?

We use BIM models to create more accurate, detailed budgets, enabling cost reduction, better planning, and higher predictability.

How does a BIM audit improve existing projects?

A BIM audit reviews and analyzes BIM adoption in projects already in progress. We identify errors, improvement opportunities, and optimization points to align your project with best practices.
