# FAQ

## What is data governance in construction?

It is the set of roles, standards, policies, and controls that keep project and operational data consistent, secure, and usable across teams and systems.

## Do we need to replace our systems to start?

No. Governance can start with your current toolset and evolve in phases. The goal is to reduce friction and improve trust without forcing an immediate platform overhaul.

## How fast do we see results?

Initial visibility and alignment typically improve early. Structural results come as the roadmap phases are executed and governance becomes part of the operating rhythm.

## Does governance really improve AI outcomes?

Yes. AI models depend on reliable data, clear definitions, and traceability. Governance reduces ambiguity, improves quality, and increases trust in outputs.

## Do you train internal teams?

Yes. Enablement is part of making governance sustainable, including workshops, role definition, and operating routines.
