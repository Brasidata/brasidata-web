    AI Readiness

    AI readiness starts with governance

    Before you scale AI, your organization should be able to say “yes” to these points:

    • Critical data domains have clear owners
    • Quality rules are defined and enforced
    • Metadata and lineage are documented
    • Access, security, and compliance are explicit
    • AI use cases are prioritized by business value

    If any of these are not mature yet, Brasidata builds a practical plan to close the gap.

    FAQ

    Frequently asked questions

    What is data governance in construction?
    It is the set of roles, policies, standards, and controls that keep data consistent, secure, and useful for operations, management, and innovation.

    Do we need to replace all systems to start?
    No. Brasidata starts from your current reality and evolves in phases, focused on practical impact.

    How soon do results show up?
    Typically within the first weeks you gain visibility and alignment. Structural results follow as the roadmap phases are executed.

    Does governance really help AI projects?
    Yes. Without quality, context, and traceability, AI models lose accuracy and trust.

    Do you train internal teams?
    Yes. Enablement is part of the work to ensure continuity and scale.
